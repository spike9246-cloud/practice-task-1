The University of Chakwal is a public sector university located in Chakwal, Punjab, Pakistan. It was established in 2020 to provide higher education opportunities to students of the region. The university offers programs in various disciplines including engineering, sciences, social sciences, and humanities. With modern facilities and a growing faculty, it aims to promote quality education and research. The institution plays an important role in the academic and professional development of students in the area.
